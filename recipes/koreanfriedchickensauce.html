Korean Fried Chicken Sauce

Description

Korean Fried Chicken Sauce is a sauce that is widely used and appreciate in South Korea.
It is famously served for fried chicken, but with its felxible utility, you can be as creative as you want in its use.

Ingredients

  • Gochujang 0.5
  • Brown sugar 1
  • Garlic
  • Ketchup 2
  • Mulyut 3
  • Soy sauce 1
  • Water 2
  • Olive oil3

Steps

  1. Hit up the pan in medium heat
  2. Put the ingredients except garlic in the pan acoording to the ratio next to each of them
  3. Give them a good stir
  4. Add grinded garlic as much as you want
  5. As the sauce will heat up, taste the sauce and then add the ingredient that that you think needs to be put more
  6. Wait until it will boil, and there you have it, Korean Fried Chicken Sauce!
