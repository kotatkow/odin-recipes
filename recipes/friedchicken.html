Fried Chicken

Description

Everybody loves fried chicken. And here are the ingredients and how you combine them to create a delicious and juicy fried chicken that everybody loves.

This particular recipes uses curry powder to give it an extra kick, while preparing the chicken marinated in the milk so that it will tenderise the meat and remove the smell of the chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken cuts
  • Milk
  • Curry powder
  • All purpose powder
  • Paprika powder
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Dried Oregano
  • Dried basil
  • White pepper
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Put your chickn in a bowl and add milk in it until the entire chicken will be submerged. And then cover the bowl with plastic wrap to let it sit in the refrigerator for 4 hours.
  2. Pour the vegetable oil in the skillet until you will have enough amount of it to fry your chickens and heat it to 350 degress.
  3. Prepare the dredging mixture by adding curry powder, all purpose powder, paprika powder, onion powder, garlic powder, dried oregano, dried basil, white pepper and cayenne pepper.
    Keep their ratio the same except for onion and garlic powder whose ratio will be twice as big as the others. All purpose powder needs to be in exceeding amount to the others.
  4. Remove the chicken pieces from the milk bowl and then shake off the excess.
  5. Gently coat the chicken pieces with the dredging mixture. And give them little taps to remove excess.
  6. Put 3 to 4 pieces in the oil at each time to prevent the oil's temperature to drop down significantly.
  7. Fry them in the oil for 14 minutes and then take them out to rest them on paper towel for at least 10 minutes before serving.
